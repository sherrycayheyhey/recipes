Ingredients:
 - water

Directions:
 1. Collect water in vessel with pouring spout. 
 2. Pour into popsicle mold. 
 3. Put the lid on. 
 4. Open freezer, place filled mold in there, and wait about 6 hours or overnight.
 5. Done! Enjoy your water-flavored popsicle! 

 Try using different types of water like tap from different cities, Evian, Fiji, 
 or even mountain spring!
